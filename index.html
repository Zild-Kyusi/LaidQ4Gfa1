Sign Up for LifeSigns Male Suicide Prevention Campaign

Thank you for your interest in supporting our campaign. By signing up, you will help us to raise awareness of male suicide and support those who are affected by it.

Here are three reasons why supporting our campaign is important:

  1. Male suicide is the biggest killer of men under the age of 45 in the UK.
  2. Many men are reluctant to seek help when they are struggling with their mental health, which can make them more vulnerable to suicide.
  3. By signing up and supporting our campaign, you can help us to break down the stigma surrounding mental health and encourage men to seek the help they need.
