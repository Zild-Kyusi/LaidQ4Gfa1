SIGN UP FOR LIFESIGNS MALE SUICIDE PREVENTION CAMPAIGN

Thank you for your interest in supporting our campaign. By signing up, you will help us raise awareness of male suicide and support those who are affected by it.

Here are three reasons why your support matters:

  1. Male suicide is the leading cause of death among men under 45 in the UK.
  2. Many men are hesitant to seek help for mental health struggles, increasing the risk of suicide.
  3. By signing up, your support can break down the stigma surrounding mental health and encourage men to seek the help they need.
